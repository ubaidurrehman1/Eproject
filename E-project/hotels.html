EXTENDED STAY HOTELS

Our extended hotel resort is a type of accommodation that offers guests a combination of hotel and resort amenities for an extended period of time. These properties are designed to provide a home-away-from-home experience for guests who need to stay for a week, a month, or even longer. Extended hotel resorts typically feature spacious suites or apartments that are fully furnished and equipped with a kitchen, living area, and separate bedrooms. In addition to the comfortable living space, guests can enjoy resort-style amenities such as swimming pools, fitness centers, on-site dining, and recreational activities. These resorts are often located in popular vacation destinations and cater to travelers who want the convenience of a hotel with the amenities of a resort, while also enjoying the flexibility and comfort of having their own fully-equipped living space. Extended hotel resorts are ideal for families, groups, and business travelers who need more space, privacy, and convenience than a traditional hotel can offer.
